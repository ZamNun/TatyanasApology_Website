My Apology

to: Tatyana

from: Zamir

This letter that I am writing is to be made out to the very lovely woman I have chosen to have in my life and that I prayed for. I loved you ever since
I kissed you back in CD Morris’s office at Beckwith hall. In those days I was a wide innocent new to love.
I never knew the journey me and you would take together. I never knew me and you would have the emotional bond with me and you would experience.
I love you so very much and I am sorry for all the pain I have put you through and hopefully this letter would make it all better. I do not know if you will be accepting of this letter,
this gesture or of anything I will do to make the situation better. I only hope you express my apology and maybe this will show you where I am at emotionally, spiritually,
and where I am going in the future. I am only human and I am bound to mess up but that doesn’t mean I am too proud to apologize and make it up.
And that is what I intend to do and will do going forward. Love is patient, love is kind and I seek to show you that with this letter.
I poured my heart and soul into this letter and all I am asking you to do is to read it. I developed it into a website so that you are able to look at it on your phone
without much strain on your devices and to show how much effort I am willing to put into our relationship by creating this website.
